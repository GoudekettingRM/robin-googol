What is a Googol?

Basically, a Googol is a very, very, very (I can't emphasise this enough), very large number. It is 10100. Writing it out is already a pain, but here it goes:

10,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000,​000

That wasn't so hard. It is just a 10 followed by 100 zeros. Actually it's nothing more than that, but there is a bunch of interesting information to be found on the wikipedia page. Or if you're more visually inclined, check out some videos on youtube. There is a lot!

Why would you make this??

This is a simple case of Robin found something interesting but wanted to know more and experience it better. A Googol is difficult to comprehend, because it's such a ridiculously large number. So I figured, why not try to scroll to the end*. And how better to do it than to put my own name on the screen a bunch, a Googol amount of times to be precise.

* Spoiler: I did not make it till the end, maybe you can do better than I did
